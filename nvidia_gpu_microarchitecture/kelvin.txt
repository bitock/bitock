Release date			2001-2003 2001
Fabrication process		150 nm




Kelvin is the codename for a GPU microarchitecture developed by Nvidia, and released in 2001, as the successor to the Celsius[1] microarchitecture.

It was named with reference to William Thomson, 1st Baron Kelvin and used with the GeForce 3 and 4 series.
